Best Car Wash in Los Angeles

We offer full service mobile car washes in the Los Angeles area and San Fernando Valley. We come to YOU! We use only premium soaps and tools to give your car a new shine. Also available are deep interior cleanings.

Our Services

Car Wash

We will come to you for your car wash. We ooffer all different levels of washes and we will always guarentee your car to be spotless after, or you can get your money back.

Interior Cleanings

Whether you have cloth or leather seats, we can clean them. Using high quality soaps and brushes, we will deep clean your seats and carpets. You will be shocked at the before and after. Full thorough interior details are also available.

Ceramic Coatings

Using Armor Shields Ceramic Coating, we can get rid of all the ugly swirl makes and deep paint scratches. This makes your car look better than ever! Other benefits include making your paint hydrophobic so water marks are less likely to occur and washes become easier.

About David

David is a car lover and only wants the best for you. He began loving persersing all cars and making sure everyone has a clean car when he was 10. He started DL Mobile Car Wash at 16 and has been helping the people in Los Angeles make sure their rides are spotless.
